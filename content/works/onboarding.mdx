## Overview

UPSC CSE is one of India’s most prestigious competitive exams, opening doors to top administrative, diplomatic, and policy-making roles. Its vast syllabus, rigorous selection process, and high competition make it one of the toughest exams in the country.
The exam consists of three stages:
Prelims - Objective MCQs,
Mains - Descriptive written test, and
Interview
In 2024, over 1.3 million aspirants appeared for the exam, yet only about 1–2% qualified for the Mains and just 0.2% for the Interview. The number of applicants continues to rise each year, making it even more competitive.

## Problem

UPSC aspirants struggle with scattered materials, time-consuming note-making, and no easy way to track progress. With such a vast syllabus, staying consistent becomes difficult, making preparation slow, stressful, and draining.

## Solution

An AI-powered study tool that helps aspirants to learn faster, take smarter notes, revise effectively, track their progress, and stay motivated, creating a more structured and stress-free preparation experience.

## Impact

User testing showed clear improvements in how aspirants learn, organise and revise their study materials.
