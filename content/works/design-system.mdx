## Overview

UPSC CSE is one of India’s most prestigious competitive exams, opening doors to top administrative, diplomatic, and policy-making roles. Its vast syllabus, rigorous selection process, and high competition make it one of the toughest exams in the country.
The exam consists of three stages:
Prelims - Objective MCQs,
Mains - Descriptive written test, and
Interview
In 2024, over 1.3 million aspirants appeared for the exam, yet only about 1–2% qualified for the Mains and just 0.2% for the Interview. The number of applicants continues to rise each year, making it even more competitive.
